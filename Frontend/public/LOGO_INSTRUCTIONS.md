# Logo Setup Instructions

## Place Your Logo Here

1. Save your RideHub logo as: `ridehub-logo.png`
2. Place it in this folder: `Frontend/public/`
3. The logo will automatically appear on the landing page

## Logo Requirements

- **Format**: PNG (recommended) or SVG
- **Size**: 512x512px or similar square dimensions
- **Background**: Transparent or white
- **File name**: `ridehub-logo.png` (exactly as shown)

## Logo Usage

The logo appears in two places:
1. **Navigation bar** - Top left corner (48x48px display)
2. **Footer** - Bottom of page (48x48px display)

## Alternative

If you don't have a logo yet, the page will gracefully fall back to using a car icon.
